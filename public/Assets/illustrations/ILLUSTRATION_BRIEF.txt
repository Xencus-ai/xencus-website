Xencus — Illustration brief (Hero images)

Goal
- Make each page feel like a corporate, trustworthy website.
- Each page should have a DIFFERENT hero illustration.
- Style: clean, modern, minimal, brand-friendly (Xencus brand: amber #facc48 + charcoal #151515).
- Avoid childish cartoons; prefer professional flat/vector or subtle 3D.
- Background should be mostly light so text remains readable.

General requirements
- Formats: AVIF preferred (also export WebP fallback if possible).
- Sizes: at least 2400px wide for landscape heroes; 1600px for portrait.
- Leave safe space for text (left/center), keep illustration weighted to right side when possible.
- Orientation: mention below for each page.
